Metro Transit

hello!

I’m a recent graduate of Macalester College where I studied statistics with minors in geography and creative writing. Right now, I work at Metro Transit as a Research and Analytics data science intern. At this position, I got interested in transit demand modeling and completed a statistics honors thesis on fitting hierarchical spatial Bayesian models to Twin Cities bus ridership. I’m interested in continuing work like this and in topics like spatial statistics, geographic data science, and reproducible research.

Outside of work, I love applying data science skills to new projects, like the National Gallery of Art’s first datathon. My team explored gender diversity in the Gallery’s collection and presented to Gallery curators and staff! When I’m not thinking about statistics, I love to read and write, knit, and rock climb.
